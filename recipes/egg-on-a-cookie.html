Egg On A Cookie

Description

Rated one of the breakfast meals of the year! The absolutely scrumptious egg on a cookie.

Heres everything you need to get started.

  • Raw egg
  • Your choice of cookie
  • Stick of butter

Lucky for you, This recipe can't be any simpler!

  1. Add three cookies on a plate
  2. Crack a raw egg on top
  3. add a few slices of butter

Bone apple teeth. A breakfast fit for a king.
