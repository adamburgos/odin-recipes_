Battery Sandwich

Description

This sandwich is an southern american classic. Super easy recipe. Super easy vibes.

This is everything you need

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 8 to 12 AA batteries (use AAA for sweeter taste)

And thats all you need! Nothing beats an easy to make home meal

In case you need instructions on how to build this, heres a step by step breakdown.

  1. Place a slice of bread on a plate
  2. Place 8 to 12 AAA batteries on the bread
  3. Put another slice of bread on top

And thats that! Enjoy your meal!
