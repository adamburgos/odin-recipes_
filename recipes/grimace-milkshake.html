Grimace Milkshake!

Description

This milkshake was made to celebrate the birthday of the Mcdonalds character Grimace! Were certain you'll enjoy our new recipe.

Now here's everything you need

  • Blender
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Milk
  • Plutonium-239
  • Blueberry syrup
  • Strawberry syrup

Now here's the recipe!

  1. Add 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream into blender
  2. Add 1/2 cups of milk
  3. Add 1/2 tablespoons of Blueberry and Strawberry syrup
  4. Sprinkle in 192 grams of Plutonium-239
  5. Blend till smooth

Serve in a cup of your coice and add whip cream. You're done!
